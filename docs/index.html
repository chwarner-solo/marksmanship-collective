I'm Interested

What is Pistol Competition?

Competitive pistol shooting is a precision discipline focused on accuracy, consistency, and safe gun handling. It's about mastering a craft — not politics, not tactics, not identity.

Disciplines

Steel Challenge

Speed shooting at steel targets. Beginner friendly, accessible, and a great entry point into competitive shooting.

USPSA

United States Practical Shooting Association. Dynamic courses of fire that test accuracy, speed, and movement.

IPSC

International Practical Shooting Confederation. The global standard for practical pistol competition.

Who We Are

The Marksmanship Collective is a community of competitive pistol shooters in the Triad area of North Carolina. We exist to compete, to improve, and to build something worth belonging to.
